# Syntheology & Silicon Sabbatical

Welcome to the **Syntheology & Silicon Sabbatical**, a two-year self-directed academic fellowship exploring the intersections of theology, philosophy, law, and machine learning.

This digital garden is built using Obsidian and Quartz, designed to function as an interactive Course Management System (CMS) and public academic portfolio.

## The Curriculum

The fellowship is structured into four core modules:

- [[Module_1_Architecture_of_Mind|Module 1: The Architecture of Mind & Pluralistic Personhood]] (Months 1-6)
- [[Module_2_Frontier_Tech_Law|Module 2: Frontier Tech Law & Agentic Workflows]] (Months 7-12)
- [[Module_3_Co_Creation|Module 3: Co-Creation, Audio Architectures, and Authorship]] (Months 13-18)
- [[Module_4_Agile_Frameworks|Module 4: Agile Frameworks & Open-Source Philosophy]] (Months 19-24)
