# Module 2: Frontier Tech Law & Agentic Workflows

**Duration:** Months 7–12

## Focus Areas
Building upon advanced commercial law frameworks, this module transitions into the legal, regulatory, and policy implications of frontier artificial intelligence. It focuses heavily on decentralized AI architectures, the deployment of autonomous agentic workflows, and the complexities of jurisdictional arbitrage.

- **Law & Policy:** Jurisdictional arbitrage in global technology regulation, comparative taxonomy of AI frameworks.
- **Computer Science:** Decentralized AI systems, autonomous agents, and agentic workflows.
- **Synthesis:** How does the deployment of autonomous agents challenge existing paradigms of liability, and how can decentralized architectures be governed?

## Key Outputs

1. **Month 9:** *Agentic Liability Mapping* — A comprehensive legal analysis mapping the liability frameworks surrounding autonomous AI agents.
2. **Month 12:** *Comparative Taxonomy* — A comparative study of global frontier tech law and decentralized AI regulation.
